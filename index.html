About Us

Excellence in Neuromonitoring

Vertebral Neurodiagnostic Associates LLC is a leading provider of intraoperative neuromonitoring (IONM) services. Our team of experienced neurophysiologists and technologists work closely with surgical teams to ensure the highest level of patient safety during complex surgical procedures.

We specialize in real-time monitoring of the nervous system during surgery, helping surgeons make informed decisions and minimize the risk of neurological complications. Our commitment to excellence, cutting-edge technology, and compassionate care sets us apart in the field.
